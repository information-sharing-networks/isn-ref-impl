About this ISN mirror

An Ecosystem of Trust (EoT) reference example of a mirror site which is part of an Information Sharing Network (ISN).

What it does

This mirror is part of an EoT Border Trade Demonstrator (BTD) ISN. It gathers signals of various categories which are syndicated to it by ISN participants to simplify the process for participants looking to subscribe to insight published by others.

A number of mirrors may be established as part of an ISN to collect signals for various purposes. Mirrors may also act as a store for signal information ensuring redundancy and enduring availability where it is necessary.

For more information on how the ISN works please see the documentation.
